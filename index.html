Home of the 6x F1 Constructors' Champions

Oracle Red Bull Racing has won six Constructors' Championships and eight Drivers' Championships with drivers Sebastian Vettel and Max Verstappen.

Max Verstappen's Championships

Max Verstappen won his first championship in 2021, at the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, beating seven time world champion Lewis Hamilton on the final lap. He became the first Dutch driver to win the title.
Verstappen won his second title in 2022, at the Japanese Grand Prix, with four races remaining in the season. His set a new record for most wins in a single season.
Verstappen secured his third consecutive F1 Championship in 2023, at the Qatar Grand Prix, with a performance that broke multiple long-standing records for dominance in a single season.
Verstappen secured his fourth consecutive title with nine Grand Prix wins, at the Las Vegas Grand Prix, including a highly praised wet-weather drive in São Paulo where he went from P17 to P1.
